Idea


Tackling a problem

On one of my long commute's back from UBC I was talking to my friend Pedram of ideas to fix current problems, and he mentioned he had this idea of making an app that would sort out when you are free to meet with your teammates when doing a group project. Initially I did not take serious this idea, due to the fact that my experience's in group projects had been quite sastisfactory. A couple of week's later I was frustrated on how I never coincided with my friends for lunch or to catch up. And that same day I met with Pedram, the one of the idea and Mattias, the experience programmer, to plan how to execute this idea.

Althought, we found an app similar to the idea of ours Mattias said some motivating words that kept us forward with the idea; He said "in today's world its hard being creative or having an original idea due to the great population, yet what makes a difference between your idea and someones idea, is your desire to suceed and the touch you add to it"

Approach


Approaching the idea

We began meeting on saturdays to plan and design out app idea, yet due to the schoolwork we faced, we decided to go 100% on this app idea till the summer.

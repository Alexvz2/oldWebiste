Summary


Steps to flip motorcycles

In 2016, together with my friend, we planned on buying a motorcycle, fixing it and then selling it to gain profit and experience. No sales experience, no mechanical repair experience, we decided to buy an old malfunctioning motorcycle. We kept a spreadsheet of all our expenses and studied the market thoroughly until finding the motorcycle we were looking for. After a month of trying to make it run without success, we realized that we had bought a really complex and complicated bike. What we had was no simple old bike, but the technological innovation of that time, with diverse sensors and electrical functions. After 3 long months of constant work on the bike, through Youtube, the manual and motorcycle forums, we were able to make the bike run and function outstandingly. Eventually, we posted it online and got diverse offers. This experience taught me many notions. Firstly, that your only limit is yourself. You can learn and do anything as long as you believe in yourself. Secondly, hard and proper work always pays off, and thirdly, that although making money can be hard, all you need is the will to get out of the comfort zone and do it.
